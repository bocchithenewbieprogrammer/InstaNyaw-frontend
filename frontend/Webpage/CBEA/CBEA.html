A Prayer for Protection and Calm during this Weather
Heavenly Father, We come before You with humble hearts as we face the weight of this present weather. The skies are heavy, the winds are strong, and the rain pours without rest. But we take refuge in You, our shelter in every storm.

Lord, we ask for Your divine protection over our families, our homes, our communities, and all who are vulnerable at this time. Keep us safe from floods, landslides, accidents, and harm. Comfort those who have been affected, and give them strength and hope in the face of difficulty.

May this weather pass without further destruction. Calm the winds, still the rains, and bring back the warmth of the sun in Your perfect time. In this season of uncertainty, help us to trust in You more deeply, serve one another more selflessly, and remain anchored in Your peace.

We pray for our leaders and frontliners, guide them with wisdom and courage. For those in need, let help reach them swiftly. And for all of us, may this be a reminder of our dependence on You, the Creator of all things.
